Empower your career and make a difference in someone else's. Welcome to the Houston Council of Women in Tech's Mentorship Program, where we believe that mentorship is a powerful tool for inspiring growth and change in the tech industry.

Benefits for Mentors:

Mentor Benefits
Give Back: Mentoring a young woman in tech is an opportunity to make a lasting impact and give back to the community.
Expand Your Network: Mentors will have the chance to meet and connect with a diverse group of talented women in tech, expanding their professional network.
Develop Leadership Skills: Mentoring others is a great way to develop leadership skills, improve communication, and enhance your ability to inspire and motivate.
Stay Current: Mentors will have the chance to stay current with the latest trends and developments in the tech industry, and to pass that knowledge on to their mentee.
Fulfillment: Mentors will experience a sense of fulfillment and satisfaction by helping someone else reach their goals and succeed in their career.

Benefits for Mentees:

Mentee Benefits
Personalized Support: With a mentor by your side, you'll have access to personalized support, guidance, and advice as you navigate your tech career.
Career Growth: Mentees will have the opportunity to grow their careers, expand their skills and knowledge, and receive support and guidance from a seasoned tech professional.
Networking Opportunities: Our mentorship program provides mentees with access to a network of successful tech professionals, allowing them to make meaningful connections and expand their professional network.
Skills Development: Mentors will work with mentees to identify areas for growth and help them develop the skills they need to succeed in the tech industry.
Empowerment: Our program is designed to empower women in tech and promote diversity and inclusion in the industry.

Attributes of a Good Mentor:

Experienced:

Ideal mentors have significant experience in the tech industry and a strong track record of success.

Supportive:

Mentors should be supportive, encouraging, and motivated to help their mentee succeed.

Good Communicator:

Mentors should have excellent communication skills and be able to effectively guide and advise their mentee.
