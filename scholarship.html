The Houston Council of Women in Tech is dedicated to empowering women in technology-related fields and shaping the future of the tech industry. We are working towards launching our Scholarship Program, in partnership with our corporate sponsors. This is designed to provide support to low income high school and college girls pursuing technology degrees as well as to provide opportunities for continuing education. With our scholarship program, we aim to not only financially support these women but also provide them with a community of like-minded individuals and opportunities for professional development. By investing in these women, we are not only helping them reach their goals but also positively influencing the future of the tech industry in Texas.

Benefits for Scholars

  1. Financial Support: Our scholarship program will provide financial assistance to women who are pursuing careers in technology. With this support, they can focus on their education and future career without worrying about the financial burden.
  2. Networking Opportunities: As a recipient of our scholarship, you will have the opportunity to join the Houston Council of Women in Tech and network with other women in technology. This community provides a supportive environment where you can share your experiences, gain insights, and form meaningful connections.
  3. Professional Development: Our scholarship program will provide opportunities for professional development. Our WiTT Member Professional Development Program, for instance, offers financial support for professional development activities such as training, certification, and conference registration.

Qualifications

  • Our scholarship program will be open to high school and college girls, as well as women who are returning to school and pursuing careers in technology.
  • Recipients must be residents of Houston, Texas.
  • Passion for Technology: To be considered for our scholarship program, you must be passionate about technology and have a genuine interest in pursuing a career in the field.
  • Preference given for low-income students and first generation college graduates. If you believe you have what it takes to be a part of this program, we encourage you to apply today. Help us shape the future of the tech industry and empower women in technology.
